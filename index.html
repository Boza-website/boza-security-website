About Boza Security Systems

Boza Security Systems Accredited Technicians (PTY) LTD has been providing professional security installations and maintenance since 2014. We are committed to protecting homes, businesses and commercial properties with reliable, high-quality security solutions.

Our experienced technicians are trained to deliver neat, professional installations using trusted brands such as Hikvision and Centurion Systems. Customer satisfaction, quality workmanship and reliable after-sales support are at the heart of everything we do.
