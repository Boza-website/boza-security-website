About Boza Security

Boza Satellite And Security Accredited Technicians (PTY) LTD has been delivering professional security installations and maintenance services since 2014. We specialise in protecting homes, businesses, body corporates and commercial properties with reliable security solutions tailored to each client's needs.

Our experienced technicians install and maintain CCTV systems, Ajax alarm systems, Hikvision cameras, electric fences, gate motors, garage door motors, intercoms, access control systems and more. We are committed to quality workmanship, excellent customer service and long-term client relationships.
