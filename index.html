About Boza

Boza Satellite And Security Accredited Technicians (PTY) LTD was registered in 2013 and has proudly served domestic and commercial customers since 2014.

We specialize in CCTV Systems, Alarm Systems, Electric Fencing, Gate Motors, Garage Door Motors, Satellite Installations and Security System Maintenance for domestic and commercial customers throughout Johannesburg and surrounding areas.

Our technicians regularly attend manufacturer training to stay up to date with the latest security technologies.
